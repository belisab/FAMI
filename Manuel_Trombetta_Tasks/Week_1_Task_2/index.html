University of Naples "L'Orientale"

The University of Naples L'Orientale is one of the oldest universities in Europe
It is located in Naples, Italy.

About the University:

L'Orientale is mainly focused on languages, cultures, and international studies.
Students can study European, Asian, African, and Middle Eastern languages.
